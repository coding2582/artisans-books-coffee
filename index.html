Our story

"A place to go and just be. There is no hurry here."

Artisans is a family project. It started as a Girl Scout idea for Emma Kate Ramsey and her mom Judi, grew into Artisans Mercantile in downtown Snohomish, and in 2022 found a new home in Everett's historic Hodges Building, first raised in 1924.

They took over two adjacent storefronts and did much of the renovation by hand. Today one side is a gift shop and the other is this bookstore and cafe, together supporting more than 100 local independent artists and authors.
